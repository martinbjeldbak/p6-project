The following two books suggest more than two parents give better quality
chromosomes when recombining genes.

Eiben, A. E. et al (1994). "Genetic algorithms with multi-parent recombination".
PPSN III: Proceedings of the International Conference on Evolutionary
Computation. The Third Conference on Parallel Problem Solving from Nature:
78–87. ISBN 3-540-58484-6.

Ting, Chuan-Kang (2005). "On the Mean Convergence Time of
Multi-parent Genetic Algorithms Without Selection". Advances in Artificial
Life: 403–412. ISBN 978-3-540-28848-0.
